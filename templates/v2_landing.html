Cost tracking

Know your real margin.

Stop guessing what you paid. RoboReseller tracks every cent — from the lot you bought at 3 AM to the platform fees deducted at sale.

  • Tax-ready exports. One click for Schedule C, one click for QuickBooks.

Inventory

Find anything in seconds.

Storage locations, batch organization, smart search. Stop tearing apart your warehouse looking for the item that just sold.

  • Storage locations. Tag every item with shelf, bin, closet, or custom location.
  • Smart search. Find by title, SKU, brand, location, or batch — across thousands of items.
  • Batch organization. Group by source, supplier, or session. Save batches as named folders.
  • Sale sync. Auto-mark items sold when eBay or Shopify orders come in. No double-listing.
Barcodes & labels

Print-ready labels for every item.

Every listing gets a unique QR + SKU code. Print sheets of labels, stick them on items, scan to find. Inventory operations like a real warehouse.

  • Auto-generated SKUs. RR-12345-SHELF-B12 format. Includes location.
  • Print sheets. Avery-compatible label templates. Print 30 at a time.
  • Scan to find. Phone camera reads QR, opens listing. Mark sold or relocated in 2 taps.
  • Pick lists. When orders come in, get a sorted pick list by location.
