Quick Start Guide

Get your first listings up in under 10 minutes

1

Open the Dashboard

After clicking "Go to Dashboard" you'll land on your main workspace. This is where all your scanned items live.

app.reselljunkie.com
2

Upload Your First Item

Click the Upload tab in the left sidebar. Select New or Used condition, then tap Start Batch. Take or upload photos of your item — multiple angles work best.

Tip: More photos = better AI identification
3

Wait for AI Processing

Our AI will identify the item and look up current market pricing automatically. This takes 30–60 seconds. You'll see it appear in your dashboard when ready.

Watch the processing queue at the top of the screen
4

Review & Edit Listings

Click any listing card to open it. You can edit the title, price, condition, and description before exporting. The AI gives you a starting point — you're always in control.

Use the ↺ ↻ buttons to rotate photos if needed
5

Export to eBay

When you're ready, click ⬇ eBay CSV at the bottom of the sidebar. Upload the downloaded file directly to eBay's bulk listing tool to publish everything at once.

eBay → Seller Hub → Listings → Add listings with file
